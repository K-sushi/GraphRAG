The healthcare industry is experiencing a digital revolution. Electronic health records (EHRs) have streamlined patient data management, while telemedicine platforms enable remote consultations. AI-powered diagnostic tools are helping doctors detect diseases earlier and more accurately. Radiology AI can identify tumors in medical scans, while machine learning algorithms analyze patient data to predict health risks. Wearable devices and IoT sensors continuously monitor vital signs, providing real-time health insights. Robotics assist in complex surgeries, improving precision and reducing recovery times. These technological advances are improving patient outcomes while reducing healthcare costs.
