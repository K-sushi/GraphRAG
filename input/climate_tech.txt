Climate change poses significant challenges to global sustainability. Renewable energy technologies like solar and wind power are becoming more efficient and cost-effective. Energy storage solutions, particularly lithium-ion batteries, are enabling grid-scale deployment of renewable energy. Smart grids use AI to optimize energy distribution and reduce waste. Carbon capture and storage technologies are being developed to remove CO2 from the atmosphere. Electric vehicles are replacing traditional combustion engines, reducing transportation emissions. Green hydrogen production offers a clean fuel alternative for heavy industry. Climate tech startups are attracting billions in investment to develop innovative solutions for environmental challenges.
